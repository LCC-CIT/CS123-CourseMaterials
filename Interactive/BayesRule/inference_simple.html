Bayes Classifier: Inference (Simpler Explanation)

Inference is the step where the classifier finally makes a choice. The decision threshold is the line it uses to make that choice. For example, if the score is above 50%, it says "yes," and if it's below 50%, it says "no." The threshold is the minimum score needed to make a final decision.
