Bayes Classifier: Probability vs. Odds (Simpler Explanation)

  • Probability is a percentage or fraction that tells you how likely something is to happen out of all possible things. (Like a 50% chance of rain).
  • Odds is a ratio that compares how many times something will happen to how many times it won't. (Like 1 to 1 odds of rain). Think of it as: "chances for" versus "chances against."
