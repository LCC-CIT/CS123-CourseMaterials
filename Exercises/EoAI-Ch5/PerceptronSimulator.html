Perceptron Simulator

This web app simulates a classifier for smiley faces. You can assign weights to the input pixels in the image by clicking on them. Clicking once sets the weight to 1, and clicking again sets it to -1. Weights of 1 will activate a happy face and weights of -1 will activate a sad face.
